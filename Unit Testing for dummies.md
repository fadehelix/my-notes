## TDD the RITE Way
Ref: https://medium.com/javascript-scene/tdd-the-rite-way-53c9b46f45e3

_RITE_ is an acronym to help you remember some important points to keep in mind. Tests should be:

-   **R**eadable
-   **I**solated OR **I**ntegrated
-   **T**horough
-   **E**xplicit
